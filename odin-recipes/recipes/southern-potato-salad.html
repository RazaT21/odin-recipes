Southern Potato Salad Recipe


This is a basic potato salad recipe which consists of a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • half cup of mayonnaise
  • half celery stalk, chopped
  • quarter cup sweet relish
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, after 15 minutes drain and chop.
  2. At the same time, place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil; cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water; peel and chop.
  3. Combine cooked potatoes and eggs with mayonnaise, celery, relish, garlic, mustard, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Gently mix together and serve warm.
